**VISION**

Aspiring Leaders, Individuals of Character  

  

**MISSION**

To inspire and develop Riverlites to be a servant leader and self-disciplined learner.&nbsp;

  

**OBJECTIVES**

  

**Student Leadership**: To develop students into concerned citizens who are willing to lead by serving and contributing to the community. &nbsp;&nbsp;

  

**Student Discipline**:&nbsp;

To inculcate in students a sense of responsibility to self and others and to instil motivation and self-discipline into them so that they can become caring and empathetic citizens who will make a difference to the community.

#### Student Leadership Experience (SLE)
-----------------------------------

  
Rivervale Primary School has a 6-year Student Leadership Experience (SLE) plan for developing student leaders in school. The SLE is aligned with the school mission to inspire and develop Riverlites to be responsible citizens and passionate learners. Anchored by our school values, the SLE sought to synergise strategically among various curricular and co-curricular programmes; allowing the school to leverage on school’s key programmes, to enhance the different aspects of the leadership experience for our students.  
  

**The Rivervale Primary School Leadership Approach  
**The school adopts the “Leading by Serving” approach which showcases that “_a servant leader is a servant first and it begins with a natural feeling that one wants to serve, to serve first. Then the conscious choice brings one to aspire to lead_.” (Robert Greenleaf, 1970)  
  

The qualities of Servant Leadership are simplified into the 3Cs (Confidence, Commitment and Compassion) which are in-lined with our school values and aim to develop SEL competencies in our Riverlites. The desired outcomes of a student leader are:  
  

“_Every student is a Confident individual and a Committed Leader who shows Compassion for Others_.”

  

The school aims to infuse the inculcation of the 3Cs through various school level programmes, specialised student leadership programmes, Co-Curricular Activities (CCA) and Values-in-Action Programmes.&nbsp; Riverlites are exposed and allowed to exercise leadership across the academic and non-academic domains. The infusion of leadership development across the school programmes is to ensure the sustainability and vibrancy of the student leadership culture within the school.
